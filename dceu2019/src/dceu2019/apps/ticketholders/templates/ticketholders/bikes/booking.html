Submit bike preference

If you would like to commute by bike, you can reserve a bike preference for easy pickup and drop-off at the Baisikeli shop, close to the venue (a 15 minutes walk). They are a socio-economic company doing great things, and they will find a bike that fits you and help you get the seat adjusted.

Confirmations will be submitted on Sunday March 31st 00:00 CET.

The actual payment is done through cash or credit card at the shop.
